    • André Sant'Anna

    Starting in February 2019, I will be a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Centre for Philosophy of Memory at the Université Grenoble Alpes in France. Prior to that, I was a Teaching Fellow in Philosophy at Massey University (July to December 2018) and I completed my PhD in Philosophy at the University of Otago (October 2018).

    My research interests are in philosophy of mind and philosophy of psychology, more specifically on questions pertaining to the relationship between episodic memory and different forms of mental time travel, the nature of mnemonic and perceptual representations, and the nature of the objects of memory.

    A more detailed description of my research topics can be found in my Research page.
