I'm a Humboldt Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Cologne Center for Contemporary Epistemology and the Kantian Tradition (CONCEPT) at the University of Cologne. Starting in Sep 2023, I'll be an SNSF Ambizione Fellow in the Department of Philosophy at the University of Geneva.

I'm also an Affiliated Member of the Centre for Philosophy of Memory at the Université Grenoble Alpes.

From 2020-2022, I was a McDonnell Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Philosophy and the Philosophy-Neuroscience-Psychology Program at Washington University in St. Louis.

From 2019-2020, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Centre for Philosophy of Memory at the Université Grenoble Alpes.

My research interests are in philosophy of mind and philosophy of psychology, with a particular focus on memory. In particular, I'm interested in questions pertaining to the nature of episodic memory and the relationship between memory, perception, and imagination.

I am currently the editor of the Theories of Memory category on PhilPapers.
