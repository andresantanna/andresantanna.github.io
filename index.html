I'm a McDonnell Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Philosophy and the Philosophy-Neuroscience-Psychology Program at Washington University in St. Louis.

As of Summer 2021, I will be Assistant Professor in the Institute of Philosophy, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Centre for Philosophy of Memory at the Université Grenoble Alpes (2019-2020), where I am now an Affiliated Member.

My research interests are in philosophy of mind and philosophy of psychology, with a particular focus on memory. In particular, I'm interested in questions pertaining to the nature of episodic memory and the relationship between memory, perception, and imagination.

I am currently the editor of the Theories of Memory category on PhilPapers.
