    • André Sant'Anna

    I am a McDonnell Postdoctoral Fellow in the Philosophy-Neuroscience-Psychology Program at Washington University in St. Louis. Before that, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Centre for Philosophy of Memory at the Université Grenoble Alpes (2019-2020), where I continue to be affiliated as an External Member.

    My research interests are in philosophy of mind and philosophy of psychology, with a particular focus on memory. In particular, I'm interested in questions pertaining to the nature of episodic memory and the relationship between memory, perception, and imagination.

    I am currently the editor of the Theories of Memory category on PhilPapers.
